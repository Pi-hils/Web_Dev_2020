Other than my passion for food, one thing I love to do is to travel.
I remember how as a kid, 6,7 years of age living in Ghana, my friends living in my neighbourhood and I would stop the games we were
playing just at the sound of a helicopter or an airplane passing up ahead. We would wave and laugh with joy, each one of
of us in their own dream land, imagining only the bravest of the brave could ever dare sit in a vehicle so alien yet so intrigueing
in the air like a bird. That was when i first fell in love with the idea of being a brave soul and travelling some day.

If you are ready, then jump into my world, my journey and my passions with me....
